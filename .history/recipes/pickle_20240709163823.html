Chamoy Pickle

Recipe Description:

There is no need for a description of this divine dish. I mean, just look at it.
From the red Takis and chili powder to the multicolor fruit roll-up, your life will truly be
changed after ingesting this beast.

Ingredients:

  • RED pickle (one that looks radioactive, preferrably)
  • Takis
  • Fruit roll-up
  • Chamoy
  • Chili powder/Tajin
  • Spaghetti candy

Steps:

  1. Obtain all ingredients
  2. Cut off the butt-end of the pickle using a knife
  3. Hollow out the RED pickle using a spoon
  4. Insert takis into hollowed out pickle, follwed with wrapping the pickle itself using the fruit roll-up
  5. Top it with spaghetti candy
  6. Drench that thing with chamoy and chili powder
  7. Enjoy!
