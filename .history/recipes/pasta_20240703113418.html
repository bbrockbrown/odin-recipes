Gigi Hadid Pasta

Recipe Description:

This was a moment back in quarantine. Everybody was going absolutely insane over how good and delicious this pasta is.

Ingredients:

  • White rice
  • Chicken
  • Beans
  • Cheese
  • Pico + veggies

Steps:

  1. Obtain all ingredients
  2. Place ingredients in a bowl, following then order above
  3. Leave as is or mix, depending on preferences
  4. Enjoy!
