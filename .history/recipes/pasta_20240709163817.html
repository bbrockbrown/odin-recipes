Gigi Hadid Pasta

Recipe Description:

This was a moment back in quarantine. Everybody was going absolutely insane over how good and delicious this pasta is.
It was THE moment. It was a culture shock. It had people quaking in their boots. Some people, to this day, still cannot comprehend
the gastronomy behind this creation. Thank you, Gigi.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups pasta of your choice (Gigi makes it with penne)
  • 12oz jar of marinara sauce
  • 12oz jar of alfredo sauce
  • 4oz mozerella and parmesean cheese
  • Parsley
  • 1 clove garlic

Steps:

  1. Obtain all ingredients
  2. Boil water and cook pasta to desired hardness
  3. Strain pasta (keep pasta water in separate bowl)
  4. Combine 8oz of marinara and 8oz of alfredo sauce into a small pot. Simmer on low heat
  5. Using strained pasta water from earlier, add 4oz of pasta water into the pot with sauces
  6. After mincing your garlic clove, add garlic to same pot. Stir until combined
  7. In a new bowl, add cooked pasta, then lather with sauce
  8. Enjoy!
